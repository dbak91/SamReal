About

The Soul Brothers Syndicate is a 20-piece band consisting of an 8-piece horn section, string quartet, 2-piece percussion section, keyboard, lead guitar, bass guitar and 3 singers.

The Soul Brothers Syndicate pay homage to Northern Soul by playing the material regularly played at Wigan Casino and other famous Northern Soul venues

Northern Soul: A Timeless Movement of Passion and Rhythm

The Soul Brothers Syndicate deliver music with sweat, style and soul. Northern Soul captures the heart of everyone with its energy and rare soul records. Reborn in the dance halls of Northern England during the late sixties, Northern Soul is a form of culture that surges with vibrance, surpassing all expectations. Northern Soul is more than a genre of music, it is a living, breathing subculture.
